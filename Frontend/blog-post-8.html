Balancing CBSE Boards and JEE Prep

Published on Feb 28, 2025 • By Ms. Kavita Joshi

The single biggest anxiety for a Class 12 science student is managing the dual pressure of scoring 95%+ in boards while securing a top rank in JEE. The secret? Realizing that they are not two different paths.

The Syllabi Overlap

Over 80% of the JEE syllabus is identical to the NCERT board syllabus. The difference is solely in the depth of application. Treat your NCERT books as your absolute foundation. You cannot solve a JEE Advanced physics problem if your core board-level theory is shaky.

The "Weekend Shift" Strategy

During the week (Monday to Friday), focus heavily on objective, competitive-level problem solving (JEE). On weekends, shift your focus to subjective writing. Dedicate Saturdays to deriving formulas step-by-step and writing out descriptive answers exactly as a board examiner expects.

Do Not Ignore English and Physical Education

Many JEE aspirants ignore their 4th and 5th subjects until February, leading to a massive panic that derails their JEE Mains preparation. Dedicate just 2 hours a week to English and your optional subject starting in October. It will save you immense stress later.
